Product Information

IBON offers a versatile range of dimensions tailored for modern, smart kitchens and premium vanity spaces. Our quartz sinks feature a robust 10mm thickness, ensuring a solid, noise-dampening, and heavy-duty feel.

Configurations: Choose the perfect fit for your workflow with our Single Bowl, Single Bowl with Drain, and Double Bowl models.

Installation Options: Adaptable to your exact design needs, our sinks support Under Mount, Flush Mount, and Top Mount installations.

Signature Finishes: Available in Snowy White, Silver Grey, Creamy Ivory, and Rosy Pink.

Packaging details

Our wash basins and quartz sinks are packaged with the utmost care to ensure they reach you in flawless condition. We use high-quality, impact-resistant materials and secure boxing techniques to prevent any damage during transit and storage.

Protective Layering: Each basin is carefully wrapped in scratch-resistant materials to protect the premium granite or metallic finish.

Custom-Fit Cushioning: We utilize custom-molded foam and heavy-duty corrugated inserts to prevent movement and absorb shocks for items weighing up to 22.5 kgs.

Secure Outer Cartons: Heavy-grade, moisture-resistant exterior boxes are clearly marked with handling instructions and included accessory details (like the waste coupling and pipe) for safe logistics.
